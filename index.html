A Proposal Regarding the Issue of Poor Water Quality Within Kosovo. by Efe
01 Abstract
Kosovo faces significant challenges related to poor water quality, impacting public health and economic development. This paper explores the root causes of this issue, including infrastructure deficiencies and regulatory shortcomings. It proposes a strategy to improve water quaity in kosovo.
Causes of Poor Water Quality in Kosovo
Aging Infrastructure: Outdated water treatment plants and leaky pipes. Pollution: Industrial activities, agriculture, and untreated sewage. Weak Regulation: Insufficient environmental regulations and monitoring. Climate Change: Droughts and floods. Financial Constraints: Limited funds for infrastructure upgrades.
